The History 📚

“Sidney Salvage” is a beautiful Second Empire style brick home that has a rich and intriguing history. Built in 1884, it has been witness to the many changes that have taken place in the neighborhood over the years.

During the early 1900s, the home was purchased by a prominent dentist who used it as his private practice. Patients would come from all over the city to visit him in his elegant office, where he provided top-quality medical care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

Bought by current owners at the end of 2020, the home is being renovated and restored to its original grandeur. With its rich history and stunning architecture, this structure is a true gem of the St. Louis neighborhood, and it will undoubtedly continue to be an important part of the city's history for many years to come.
